How to create your own WhatsApp group invite link

Got a group you'd like listed? You don't need to be a tech expert — WhatsApp builds the invite link for you in under a minute.

On your phone

Open the group → tap the group name → tap Invite to Group via Link → tap Copy Link. That's the link you'll paste into the submission form below.

Keep it working

Invite links stop working if you reset them, if the group hits 256 members, or if you turn on Approve New Members. If your listed group suddenly stops accepting joins, use the report button on its card and resubmit a fresh link.
