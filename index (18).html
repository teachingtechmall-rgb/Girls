How to Write Shayari — A Beginner's Guide

  1. Pick one emotion: Great shayari always starts from one strong feeling — love, pain, anger, or joy. Decide what you actually want to say first.
  2. Don't worry about meter (behr) at first: For beginners, it matters more that the words come from the heart than that the rhythm is perfect. The meter comes naturally with practice.
  3. Use simple words: The best shayari stays close to everyday language, yet still touches the heart.
  4. Two lines, one feeling: Every sher has two lines that complete a single thought — the first sets up the scene or question, the second delivers the answer or twist.
  5. Read and listen: Reading poets like Mirza Ghalib, Faiz, and Gulzar helps you understand rhythm and style.
  6. Practice with AI: If the words don't come, use the AI Shayari Generator for inspiration and shape it into your own style.
