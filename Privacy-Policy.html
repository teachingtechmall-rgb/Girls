Privacy Policy

Welcome to Dosti Zone.

Ye Privacy Policy page is liye banaya gaya hai taake aapko clearly samajh aa sake ke jab aap hamari website use karte hain to hum kis type ki information collect karte hain, uska kya use hota hai aur hum aapki privacy ko kaise protect karte hain.

Hum apne users ki privacy ko bohat seriously lete hain. Hamara goal sirf ek safe, transparent aur trustworthy online friendship platform banana hai jahan users bina kisi tension ke explore kar saken.

Agar aap Dosti Zone use kar rahe hain to iska matlab hai ke aap is Privacy Policy ko samajh kar us se agree karte hain.

1. Information Collection

Jab aap hamari website visit karte hain, to hum kuch basic information collect kar sakte hain jo website ko improve karne ke liye use hoti hai.

Is information mein shamil ho sakta hai:

  • Browser type aur device information
  • IP address (general location estimation ke liye)
  • Pages jo aap visit karte hain
  • Time spent on website
  • Referral source (aap website tak kaise pohanche)

Ye sab data sirf analytics aur website improvement ke liye use hota hai. Hum kisi bhi user ki personal identity ko directly identify karne ki koshish nahi karte.

2. Personal Information

Dosti Zone directly aapse koi sensitive personal information jaise CNIC, bank details ya private documents collect nahi karta.

Agar future mein hum contact form ya newsletter use karte hain, to us case mein aap se sirf basic information li ja sakti hai jaise email address ya name, jo sirf communication ke liye use hogi.

Hum aapki personal information ko kabhi bhi sell, rent ya misuse nahi karte.

3. Cookies Policy

Hamari website cookies use kar sakti hai taake user experience improve ho sake.

Cookies ek chhoti file hoti hai jo aapke browser mein save hoti hai aur website ko help karti hai ke wo aapki preferences ya activity ko yaad rakh sake.

Cookies ka use hum in purposes ke liye karte hain:

  • Website performance improve karna
  • User experience ko smooth banana
  • Analytics aur traffic analysis

Aap chahein to apne browser settings se cookies disable bhi kar sakte hain, lekin is se website ka experience thoda affect ho sakta hai.

4. Third-Party Services

Dosti Zone par kuch third-party services use ho sakti hain jaise analytics tools, advertising networks ya external links.

Ye third-party services apni apni privacy policies follow karti hain aur hum unke data collection process par direct control nahi rakhte.

Hum recommend karte hain ke jab aap kisi external link par click karein, to us website ki privacy policy zaroor read karein.

5. Advertising

Hamari website par ads show ho sakte hain jo third-party ad networks ke through display hote hain.

Ye ads user interest ke base par bhi ho sakte hain. Advertising partners cookies use kar sakte hain taake relevant ads show kiye ja saken.

Hum kisi bhi advertiser ke content ya claims ko fully control nahi karte.

6. Data Security

Hum apni website ko secure rakhne ke liye best possible steps lete hain.

Lekin internet par koi bhi system 100% secure nahi hota. Is liye hum guarantee nahi de sakte ke data completely hack-proof hoga, lekin hum regular updates aur security measures implement karte rehte hain.

Users ko bhi advise kiya jata hai ke wo apni personal information online share karte waqt caution rakhein.

7. External Links

Hamari website par kabhi kabhi external websites ke links ho sakte hain.

Hum un external websites ke content ya privacy practices ke responsible nahi hain.

Agar aap kisi external link par jate hain, to aap apni responsibility par jate hain.

8. Children’s Privacy

Dosti Zone kisi bhi 13 saal se chhote bachon se knowingly personal information collect nahi karta.

Agar aap ek parent ya guardian hain aur aapko lagta hai ke aapke child ne hamare platform par koi information share ki hai, to aap humse contact kar sakte hain.

Hum turant action lene ki koshish karenge aur zaroori ho to data remove kar diya jayega.

9. Consent

Hamari website use karte hue aap is Privacy Policy se agree karte hain.

Agar aap is policy se agree nahi karte, to hum aapko advise karte hain ke website ka use na karein.

Website continue use karna iska matlab hai ke aap hamari policies ko accept karte hain.

10. Updates to This Policy

Hum kabhi bhi is Privacy Policy ko update kar sakte hain.

Changes hone par updated version isi page par publish kiya jayega.

Hum recommend karte hain ke aap time time par is page ko check karte rahein taake aapko latest updates ka pata chalta rahe.

11. User Responsibility

Har user ki responsibility hai ke wo website ko safe aur respectful tareeke se use kare.

Hum expect karte hain ke users kisi bhi fake, spam ya harmful activity se door rahenge aur community ko positive banayenge.

12. Final Words

Dosti Zone ka main goal ek simple aur friendly online environment create karna hai jahan log apas mein connect ho saken.

Hum aapki privacy ki respect karte hain aur usko protect karne ke liye committed hain.

Aapka trust hamare liye sab kuch hai.

Agar aapko is Privacy Policy ke baare mein koi sawal ho, to aap humse contact kar sakte hain.

Shukriya ke aap Dosti Zone ka hissa hain.
